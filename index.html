The Truth About Medicare

It is common to feel like a fish out of water when navigating Medicare. Though popular and useful to millions of seniors, there are many people – beneficiaries and prospects alike – who have varied misconceptions about the nationwide health insurance program.

Besides these fallacies, there are some critical aspects of Medicare that people are simply not aware of. Let’s clear the fog on Medicare, addressing the myths and misconceptions to help you make better and informed decisions when enrolling in Medicare.
